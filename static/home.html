What Is This Project

Cars are one of the bigger purchases we make in our lives, and we believe it is important to make these decisions with knowledge. We aimed to create a tool to view the differences between different car models in a simple way.




Environmental Considerations

Cars are a major part of our carbon footprint, and we believe informed purchases of (relatively) environmentally friendly cars can have a dramatic impact on reducing our carbon footprint.
